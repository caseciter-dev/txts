O R D E R
1. By filing this transfer petition, the petitioner-wife seeks
transfer  of  Divorce  Petition  instituted  by  the  respondent-
husband bearing H.M.A. No.815 of 2025 titled “ Chakit Gupta
vs. Anukrati Agrawal ” from the court of the Principal Judge,
Family Court, Shahdara District, Karkardooma Courts to the
Family Court, District Gautam Buddha Nagar, Uttar Pradesh.
The ground urged is that the petitioner-wife has a child to look
after and that it would be impossible for her to contest the
divorce suit in the court at New Delhi. 
2. The transfer petition is indeed frivolous and filed in

--- Page 6 ---
abuse of the process of law. The distance between the two
courts is barely 16 kms. (approximately); not only that, the
distance between the residence of the petitioner-wife and the
Family  Court,  Gautam  Buddha  Nagar  is  20  kms.
(approximately).
3. The transfer petition  is, accordingly, dismissed with
cost of Rs.5,000/- (Five Thousand rupees) only, which shall be
made over by the petitioner-wife to the Secretary General of
this Court by Demand Draft, within a period of four weeks.
Once  the  Demand  Draft  is  made  over  and  encashed,  the
Secretary  General  shall  utilize  the  said  amount  towards
refreshments being arranged for children in an orphanage (to
be selected by him).  
4. Pending application(s), if any, shall stand disposed of.
