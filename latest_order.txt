through the material on record, we are not persuaded to
grant bail to the petitioner, for the present.
1

--- Page 14 ---
SLP (Crl.) No.12056/2026
3. Accordingly,  the  Special  Leave  Petition  stands
dismissed.
4. However, as has been pointed out by the learned
counsel for the petitioner that for over 02 years and 04
months, the petitioner is behind bars and the trial has
not begun in the sense that only charges have been framed
and out of 31 prosecution witnesses, only 03 have been
examined, despite the fact that charges were framed on
10.10.2024. This Court has regularly been coming across
cases where on the one hand, the State vehemently opposes
the prayer for bail to the accused but on the other hand,
is absolutely not bothered to proceed with the trial. The
Court cannot shut its eyes to the double standards of the
State.
5. Thus, we find it is a fit case to pass strict orders.
6. Accordingly, personal cost is imposed on the Senior
Superintendent of Police, District Amritsar of Rs.50,000/-
(Rupees Fifty Thousand) to be deposited in the Advocates’
Welfare Fund of the Local Bar Association of Amritsar
District. The same be done within two weeks.
7. Further, it is directed that the prosecution/ State
shall ensure that at least 04 witnesses are examined per
month  in  the  case  and  the  trial  is  taken  to  its
conclusion. The Trial Court is also directed to ensure
compliance of the present order.
8. Registry is directed to communicate the order to the
2

--- Page 15 ---
SLP (Crl.) No.12056/2026
Trial Court forthwith. 
9. We further indicate that in future if we come across
such cases anywhere in India, similar stringent orders
shall be passed against the person(s) responsible, more
so, the prosecution. 
10. After the order was passed, learned counsel for the
State of Punjab prayed for one opportunity to explain the
reason for the trial to proceed in such a slow manner.
Though the Court was not satisfied, but still, by way of
indulgence, for consideration on the issue of whether the
order passed against the Senior Superintendent of Police,
Amritsar is required to be reconsidered, post the matter
on 24.07.2026 on top of the list. Till then, the order at
paragraph 6 above imposing cost is kept in abeyance.
